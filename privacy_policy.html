Last updated 8/27/2022

It is an inevitability that I must collect certain information from users of NerdBot. This information includes:

  • Your Discord username
  • Your developer Discord ID (which can be publically accessed through Discord's Developer Mode)
  • Your birth month and date
  • Names and developer IDs for servers the bot is a part of

With great data requires great responsibility. With this responsibility in mind, I assure that I will not distribute or sell any data this application stores, public or private.

Registering with NerdBot grants consent to the storage of the data mentioned above. Upon unregistering, NerdBot will immediately cease to store your data until you register again.

For any concerns, please notify me. I hope that you enjoy your time with NerdBot.
